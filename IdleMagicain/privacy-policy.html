1. 簡介 (Introduction)

歡迎使用《IdleMagician》（以下簡稱「本遊戲」）。本遊戲由 RiderYue 開發與發行（以下簡稱「我們」或「開發者」）。本隱私權政策說明我們如何收集、使用、儲存和分享您的資訊，並解釋您對此的權利與選擇。

Welcome to "IdleMagician" (hereinafter referred to as "the Game"). The Game is developed and published by RiderYue (hereinafter referred to as "we" or "the Developer"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your information, as well as your rights and choices regarding your data.

2. 我們收集哪些資訊？ (What Information Do We Collect?)

(A) 廣告與分析數據 (Advertising & Analytics Data)

我們使用 Google AdMob、Unity Ads 和 ironSource LevelPlay 來支持遊戲運營，這些廣告平台可能會收集您的裝置資訊、廣告識別碼 (IDFA / GAID)、IP 地址與使用行為數據，以提供個人化廣告與優化廣告投放。

We use Google AdMob, Unity Ads, and ironSource LevelPlay to support the operation of the game. These advertising platforms may collect device information, advertising identifiers (IDFA / GAID), IP addresses, and usage behavior data to provide personalized ads and optimize ad placement.

(B) Google 登入與雲端存檔 (Google Login & Cloud Save)

如果您選擇使用 Google 登入 (OAuth 2.0) 來連結遊戲存檔，我們會收集您的 Google 使用者名稱、電子郵件地址、OAuth Token 及 UGS Player Authentication ID，並將存檔數據儲存於雲端。

If you choose to log in with Google (OAuth 2.0) to link your game save, we will collect your Google username, email address, OAuth Token, and UGS Player Authentication ID, and store the save data in the cloud.

3. 如何使用這些資訊？ (How Do We Use This Information?)

我們將這些數據用於：提供遊戲服務、顯示個人化廣告、防止詐欺與作弊、改善遊戲體驗，以及符合法規要求。

We use this data to: provide game services, display personalized ads, prevent fraud and cheating, improve the gaming experience, and comply with legal requirements.

4. 您的權利與選擇 (Your Rights & Choices)

根據 GDPR 和 CCPA，您有權選擇退出個人化廣告、請求刪除您的數據（可透過遊戲內設定完成），以及存取您的個人數據副本。然而，請注意，如果您不同意本隱私權政策，則無法使用本遊戲。

According to GDPR and CCPA, you have the right to opt out of personalized ads, request data deletion (which can be done through in-game settings), and access a copy of your personal data. However, please note that if you do not agree to this Privacy Policy, you will not be able to use the Game.

5. 我們不會發送垃圾郵件 (We Do Not Send Spam)

我們不會使用您的電子郵件發送通知，也不會寄送垃圾郵件 (Spam)。您的電子郵件僅用於身份驗證與雲端存檔。

We do not use your email to send notifications, nor do we send spam emails. Your email is only used for authentication and cloud save purposes.
